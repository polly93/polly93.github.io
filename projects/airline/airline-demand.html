TRANSPORTATION · DEMAND ANALYSIS

Domestic Airline Passenger Demand

A route-level demand study of Korean domestic aviation markets and transportation substitution.

PROBLEM

How do passengers substitute across carriers and travel modes?

The analysis studies passenger choice across domestic airlines while recognizing high-speed rail as an important outside option on inland routes.

DATA & WORKFLOW

Building an analysis-ready dataset

  • Constructed origin–destination-by-month markets.
  • Integrated passenger counts, route information, carriers, airports, and fare measures.
  • Separated Jeju and inland markets to reflect different substitution patterns.

METHOD

Discrete-choice demand estimation

Applied market-level demand methods to quantify substitution across carriers and transportation alternatives.

INTERPRETATION

What the analysis contributes

The project combines market definition, domain knowledge, large-panel construction, and econometric demand analysis.
